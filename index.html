Privacy Policy for Nova: AI Fortune Teller

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Barış Görgün collects and uses your information when you use the Nova: AI Fortune Teller mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Images: We require you to upload photos of coffee grounds to provide AI-based interpretations.
  • Usage Data: We use Google Analytics for Firebase and Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous technical data to improve app performance.

2. Data Retention

Uploaded images are stored securely on Firebase servers for a maximum of 7 days. This is strictly for troubleshooting technical errors. After this period, images are permanently deleted.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share data only with necessary service providers for app functionality:

  • Google Gemini API: For analyzing images and generating text.
  • Google Firebase: For secure data storage and crash reporting.

4. User Rights and Data Deletion

You have the right to access or delete your data at any time. To request manual deletion of your images before the 7-day period, please contact us at the email below.

5. Children's Privacy

Nova is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
