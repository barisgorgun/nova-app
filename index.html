Privacy Policy for Nova: AI Fortune Teller

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Barış Görgün collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Images: Photos of coffee grounds are uploaded to provide AI interpretations.
  • Usage Data: We use Google Analytics for Firebase to collect anonymous technical data (e.g., app version, device model).
  • Personal Data: We do not collect or store names, phone numbers, or email addresses unless you contact us voluntarily.

Uploaded images are stored securely on Firebase for a maximum of 7 days for technical analysis and interpretation quality. After this period, they are permanently deleted.

Account & Data Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of all your data. To do so, please contact us at barisgorgunn@gmail.com. Requests are processed within 24-48 hours.

To provide our services, we use trusted third-party providers. You can review their privacy policies below:
